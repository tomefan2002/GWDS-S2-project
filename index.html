Booking Info

RRR offers a number of services for our customers that decide rent the van out. Our prices are as follows:

Base price: £85/hour

(Includes all standard services, stock, hireables and more!)

Addons: custom orders of stock for gifts or otherwise are available to addon when renting out the van. This could be things like trucks, bearing or decks that the RRR van doesn't normally stock

Please see the below section to book!

Forms

In order to book out the RRR van you *must* fill out both the Booking Form *and* the Health and Safety Disclaimer – Please note *anyone* under the age of 18 must fill out their own copy of the Health and Safety Disclaimer.

Please fill out the optional third form to let RRR's owner know if you would like any addons.

Once forms are submitted please wait for an email from us within 3 working days to proceed further.
